Puka Shell Beach

Located on the northern tip of Boracay, Puka Shell Beach is a tranquil haven away from the bustling main beach. Known for its unique shoreline of crushed puka shells and golden sand, this unspoiled paradise offers a quieter, more natural vibe.

Perfect for relaxation, leisurely strolls, or simply soaking in the stunning coastal views, Puka Shell Beach is a must-visit for those seeking peace and serenity. With its crystal-clear waters and picturesque surroundings, it’s a beautiful escape that showcases Boracay’s raw and untouched beauty.

Puka Beach is a serene destination in Boracay that boasts a white-sand shore that contains bits of coral. Going on for 800 meters, it is also one of the longest beaches on the island.

While the bits of coral make its sand slightly more coarse than those of the White Beach, this spot makes up for it with its almost unspoiled natural beauty. Puka Beach is lined with tall coconut trees as well, and has a lush tropical forest just past its stunning shore.

It is also home to Boracay's flying foxes, which are a species of fruit bats. From the shore, you can spot these animals flying around at night when they are most active.

Kayaking or paddleboarding

Glide across the waters of Puka Shell Beach on a paddleboard and soak in the breathtaking scenery around you. Paddleboarding is a relaxing yet engaging activity perfect for beginners and seasoned adventurers alike. Whether you're exploring the shallow shores or venturing further out to enjoy the serenity of the open sea, it's a fantastic way to connect with the beauty of Boracay's pristine coastline.

Watch the sunset

While sunset is the busiest time on White Beach, on Puka it’s the quietest time. So if you’re looking for a romantic or quiet and peaceful sunset getaway, Puka Beach is one of, if not your best option.

Swimming

Cool off and see the clear blue water from up close. Be aware that you can only stand about 5 meters in though.

Local Restaurants

After a day of sun, sand, and sea, treat yourself to some of the best local dining experiences near Puka Shell Beach. This charming coastal area offers a variety of restaurants where you can indulge in delicious local and international cuisine while enjoying the stunning views and warm ambiance.

Whether you’re craving freshly caught seafood, traditional Filipino dishes, or something more familiar, the restaurants near Saud Beach cater to every taste. From beachfront dining with a view of the waves to cozy spots serving home-cooked meals, there’s something to satisfy every craving.

Vintana Asian Cafe at Shangri-La Boracay

Vintana Asian Café offers an exquisite dining experience with a stunning view of Boracay’s crystal-clear waters. Specializing in Asian-inspired cuisine, the café presents a blend of flavors from various Asian cultures, with an emphasis on fresh seafood and locally sourced ingredients. The open-air ambiance, coupled with the restaurant’s elegant design, creates a relaxing yet sophisticated atmosphere. Whether you're enjoying a casual meal or a special celebration, Vintana is a perfect spot to indulge in delicious food while enjoying the breathtaking sunset views.

Sirena

For a truly special dining experience, Sirena at Puka Beach offers an upscale beachfront setting with world-class seafood and a variety of gourmet dishes. Known for its romantic ambiance and stunning ocean views, Sirena serves an eclectic menu of fresh, locally inspired dishes and international cuisine. Enjoy a laid-back, yet elegant dining experience while savoring deliciously prepared seafood and signature cocktails. The soft sea breeze and serene atmosphere make Sirena a perfect place to enjoy a sunset dinner or a relaxed meal with loved ones on the beautiful Puka Beach.

Savoy Cafe

Located in the heart of Boracay, Savoy Café offers a modern dining experience with a mix of international and local Filipino flavors. Whether you’re craving freshly prepared pasta, juicy steaks, or classic Filipino comfort food, Savoy Café delivers a diverse menu to satisfy every palate. With its cozy and contemporary setting, it’s the ideal place for a casual yet satisfying meal. The restaurant also serves a variety of refreshing beverages, making it the perfect spot to unwind after a day of island adventures.
