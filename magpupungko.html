Magpupungko Beach

located in the island paradise of Siargao, is a serene escape known for its crystal-clear waters and stunning natural pools. The beach is famous for the Magpupungko Rock Pools, which are perfect for swimming during low tide, offering a picturesque spot to relax and cool off. Surrounded by lush greenery and rocky formations, the beach provides a tranquil setting away from the crowds, ideal for nature lovers and those seeking peace. Whether you're exploring the pools, enjoying the scenic beauty, or simply soaking in the calm atmosphere, Magpupungko Beach is a must-visit for anyone exploring Siargao.

Magpupungko Beach is a hidden gem located in Siargao, known for its stunning rock pools that are best visited during low tide. The beach features clear blue waters and a peaceful atmosphere, making it perfect for swimming, relaxing, and exploring its natural beauty. Surrounded by dramatic rock formations and lush greenery, Magpupungko Beach offers a tranquil escape for those looking to connect with nature and enjoy a less crowded, serene environment. It's a must-see destination for anyone visiting Siargao.

Cliff Jumping

For the more adventurous, cliff jumping at Magpupungko’s rocky formations is a thrilling activity. The natural landscape provides spots where you can safely leap into the cool waters below.

Rock Pool Swimming

The iconic Magpupungko Rock Pools are the main attraction, offering crystal-clear natural pools that are perfect for swimming. These pools are best enjoyed during low tide when they are fully exposed, allowing for a refreshing dip surrounded by stunning rock formations.

Snorkeling

Take a leisurely stroll along the soft sand of Magpupungko Beach, which is less crowded than other popular beaches. Enjoy the peaceful ambiance, look for seashells, or simply unwind by the sea.

Local Restaurants

After a day of sun, sand, and sea, treat yourself to some of the best local dining experiences near Magpupungko Beach. This charming coastal area offers a variety of restaurants where you can indulge in delicious local and international cuisine while enjoying the stunning views and warm ambiance.

Whether you’re craving freshly caught seafood, traditional Filipino dishes, or something more familiar, the restaurants near Saud Beach cater to every taste. From beachfront dining with a view of the waves to cozy spots serving home-cooked meals, there’s something to satisfy every craving.

NAGA Siargao

NAGA Siargao is a trendy, oceanfront restaurant that offers a unique fusion of local and international cuisine, with an emphasis on fresh, high-quality ingredients. Whether you’re in the mood for a seafood feast or a deliciously crafted cocktail, NAGA’s menu has something for everyone. The contemporary design, relaxed ambiance, and stunning views of the sea make it a perfect spot to enjoy a meal while watching the sunset. Known for its fresh fish dishes and creative cocktails, NAGA offers an unforgettable dining experience in Siargao.

Shaka Siargao

Located in the heart of Siargao, Shaka Siargao is a vibrant, laid-back café known for its fresh, healthy menu that perfectly complements the island’s relaxed atmosphere. Specializing in smoothie bowls, acai bowls, fresh juices, and vegan-friendly dishes, Shaka is a favorite spot for those looking to fuel up with wholesome and delicious meals. With its cozy, beachy vibe and friendly service, it’s the perfect place to unwind after a day of exploring the island or to start your morning with a nutritious boost.

Bravo Restaurant

Located near Magpupungko Beach, Bravo Restaurant is a popular dining spot offering delicious Mediterranean-inspired cuisine, fresh seafood, and flavorful local dishes. With its laid-back ambiance and scenic views, Bravo provides a relaxing environment where you can enjoy meals crafted with the finest ingredients. From fresh seafood platters to mouthwatering tapas and signature cocktails, Bravo offers a delightful culinary experience that’s perfect after a day at the beach.
