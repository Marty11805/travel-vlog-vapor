Dahican Beach

Located in Mati, Davao Oriental, Dahican Beach is a beautiful, unspoiled stretch of coastline renowned for its clear turquoise waters and consistent waves, making it a popular spot for surfers of all levels. With its golden sand, lush surroundings, and relaxed atmosphere, Dahican is perfect for both adventure and relaxation. Beyond surfing, visitors can enjoy beach activities like skimboarding, paddleboarding, or simply lounging under the sun. Its peaceful and natural charm makes Dahican Beach an ideal destination for those seeking an escape into nature while enjoying thrilling water sports.

It is a stunning coastal destination known for its pristine beauty and excellent surfing conditions. The beach stretches for several kilometers, with powdery white sand and clear blue waters that attract both local and international surfers. The area is famous for its consistent waves, making it ideal for both beginners and experienced surfers.

Beyond surfing, Dahican offers a tranquil setting with its scenic views, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and outdoor activities like skimboarding and paddleboarding. The surrounding lush forests and crystal-clear waters add to its natural allure. With fewer crowds compared to more popular beaches, Dahican Beach is a peaceful escape for those looking to connect with nature and enjoy the beauty of the ocean.

Surfing

For the more adventurous, cliff jumping at Magpupungko’s rocky formations is a thrilling activity. The natural landscape provides spots where you can safely leap into the cool waters below.

Beach Volleyball

Dahican Beach is a paradise for surfers, offering consistent and reliable waves year-round. Whether you're a beginner looking for lessons or an experienced surfer seeking bigger waves, Dahican’s gentle swells and powerful breaks cater to all levels. The beach is known for its smooth, long rides, making it a great spot to practice and improve your skills. The surf culture is vibrant, with local surf schools available to guide you through the experience, ensuring fun and safety while enjoying the waves.

Resorts hopping

Dahican Beach is home to several cozy and picturesque resorts, perfect for those looking to unwind and enjoy the laid-back atmosphere. Resort hopping is a fun way to explore the different accommodations, each offering unique features, from beachfront cottages and pools to excellent local cuisine. You can spend your day lounging at one resort, then head to another for a meal or enjoy a new view. It’s a great way to discover the charm of Dahican while experiencing the hospitality of its local resorts.

Local Restaurants

After a day of sun, sand, and sea, treat yourself to some of the best local dining experiences near Dahican Beach. This charming coastal area offers a variety of restaurants where you can indulge in delicious local and international cuisine while enjoying the stunning views and warm ambiance.

Whether you’re craving freshly caught seafood, traditional Filipino dishes, or something more familiar, the restaurants near Saud Beach cater to every taste. From beachfront dining with a view of the waves to cozy spots serving home-cooked meals, there’s something to satisfy every craving.

Ciangi

Located along the stunning Dahican Beach, Ciangi is a cozy and vibrant restaurant known for its fresh seafood and locally inspired dishes. Offering a relaxed beachfront dining experience, Ciangi serves up a variety of Filipino favorites with a modern twist, including grilled seafood, fresh fish, and delicious local snacks. The laid-back atmosphere, paired with great food and friendly service, makes it an ideal place to unwind after a day of beach activities while enjoying the ocean breeze and scenic views.

The Lokal

For a unique taste of local and international flavors, The Lokal in Dahican Beach is a must-visit. With a menu that combines Filipino comfort food and global influences, The Lokal is perfect for those looking to indulge in fresh, flavorful meals. The relaxed yet modern ambiance makes it a great spot to enjoy a leisurely lunch or dinner, with popular dishes including savory meat dishes, fresh salads, and seafood. With its vibrant atmosphere and commitment to quality, The Lokal is a favorite dining spot for both locals and tourists alike.

Paulpetz Food House - Dahican

Paulpetz Food House is a charming restaurant in Dahican Beach that offers a simple but satisfying menu with a focus on fresh and delicious Filipino comfort food. Whether you're craving grilled meats, hearty stews, or local snacks, Paulpetz delivers flavorful dishes made with care and locally sourced ingredients. Known for its casual setting and friendly service, it’s the perfect spot to enjoy an authentic and affordable meal while relaxing in the peaceful surroundings of Dahican Beach.
